# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, technically rigorous, and harassment-free community. Participants
should be constructive, assume good intent while evaluating evidence, respect privacy, and make room
for people with different backgrounds and levels of experience.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, deliberate
disclosure of private information, sexualized conduct, threats, sustained disruption, and publishing
credentials or host data without authorization.

## Project spaces

This standard applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, release channels,
and public spaces where someone is representing this project. Technical disagreement is welcome;
attacking people is not.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through the contact methods on the
[`@haoz0206`](https://github.com/haoz0206) profile. Do not use a public issue for sensitive reports.
Maintainers may edit or remove content, close or lock conversations, reject contributions, issue a
warning, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Reports will be handled as privately
and consistently as reasonably possible.
